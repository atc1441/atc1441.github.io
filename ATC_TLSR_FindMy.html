ATC_FindMy Flasher

by ATC1441

How-To(As Video here, click):

Connect a Telink TLSR8252 or other supported SoC to a USB-to-UART Converter. From Converter to SoC, connect wires as following:
VCC -> VCC
GND -> GND
TXD -> SWS
DTR or RTS -> RST(Optional)


Enter the FindMy/OpenHaystack Public key in the field,
click on Open to select your COM-Port.
Flashing will start immediately.
